The Origin Story

It was during an interview with The Beach Boys that Adam Reader earned his nickname. The legendary band was so impressed by his encyclopedic knowledge of their catalog that one member declared:

"You're the Professor of Rock — you know more about us than we remember!"

That name stuck. Today, Adam has conducted over 500 exclusive interviews with the biggest names in rock history, preserving the stories behind the soundtrack of our lives.

From his studio in Utah, he's become the go-to interviewer for rock legends — someone who asks the questions fans really want to know. Not about controversy. About the songs.

About Adam Reader

Adam Reader is a Utah-based music historian, interviewer, and the host of Professor of Rock. What started as quiz games with his father about classic rock has evolved into one of the most respected music documentary channels on YouTube.

His first major interview was with Kenny Loggins, who pulled him aside afterward and said he had a rare gift for connecting with artists. Loggins personally helped connect Adam with The Beach Boys and The Doobie Brothers.

"I am a fan just like you're a fan. It's always about the songs."
